    Full Stack Developer

    This position is focused performing incremental work on our system, and has the end-line responsibility for all bug fixes, UI/UX changes, and general updates to existing code.   Programmer will perform bug fixes, manipulate SQL data in a MySQL database, and perform general code maintenance and management tasks. The ideal candidate is an experienced professional in developing solutions to address complex database design and data modeling challenges. Documents and works on our innovative media platform.

    What We're Looking For:

    • A desire to be deeply involved in product definition. Our developers are welcomed and expected to contribute to the direction of their projects 
    • An appreciation for clean, reusable and well-tested code
    • Solid experience with PHP, WordPress and MySQL
    • Experience beyond the basic LAMP stack architecture
    • Zend or any other modern MVC framework is a must
    • Working knowledge of Responsive Design, Javascript, jQuery, HTML5, and CSS.
    • Must be a team player
    • You must understand what the company’s mission is about
    • Resourceful and self-starter
    • ETL processing (our data is growing by the hour) and experience dealing with hundreds gigabytes of data is a plus
    • Bachelor's degree, Associate's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or equivalent - and/or – Two years in a relevant industry or education 

    What We Like:

    • PHP5, ZEND framework in particular
    • MySQL, taken to its furthest extent
    • Prototype and jQuery (along with hand-crafted JS)
    • Redis, Memcached, Mongo
    • Asynchronous requests and queues (frontend and backend)
    • git and GitHub
    • SVN
